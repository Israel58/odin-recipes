Bread Twists

delicious bread twists of the highest order of yummyness, in less than 30 minutes

Ingredients you'll need:

  • Dough
  • Minced Garlic
  • Shredded cheese
  • Garlic Seasoning
  • Garlic sauce

Directions:

  1. Stretch dough and cut into 10 x 10 square, about 1/4 inch thick
  2. Coat dough lightly in garlic sauce
  3. Season with garlic rub
  4. Add 3 ounces of Shredded cheese and 1/2 ounce of minced garlic into a bowl and mix
  5. Spread mix on bottom half of dough
  6. Fold dough in half
  7. Cut into fourths, and cut those fourths into thirds
  8. Separate the strips and twist them twice
  9. Place on tray and add a coat of garlic sauce on top before placing in oven
  10. Bake for 6 minutes at 325 degrees

Pizza

This delicious pizza hails from a little town in italy, but fear not! Today we will be bringing this slice of heaven to your home, not matter where you live

Ingredients you'll need:

  • Dough
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Herb Seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Stretch out dough to approximately 10 inches
  3. Oil pan and place dough
  4. Generously cover dough with sauce
  5. Evenly spread cheese on top of sauce
  6. Season with a pinch of herbs
  7. Place in oven for 10 minutes
  8. If bubbles form, pop with fork
